Interview Questions -------------------

🧠 Q1: Why custom exceptions?
“To create meaningful, structured, and scalable error handling.”

🧠 Q2: Where to handle exceptions?
“At the layer where decisions can be made.”

🧠 Q3: Why use exception hierarchy?
“To group errors and allow flexible handling.”

🧠 Q4: What is exception chaining?
“Preserving original error while raising a higher-level exception.”
